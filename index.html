Profile

A Decade of Design,
Driven by Curiosity

For over a decade, I've worked at the intersection of brand, editorial, and digital design — guided by the conviction that how something looks is inseparable from how it makes you feel. My practice spans identity systems, art direction, and the quiet details that turn a good design into an unforgettable one.

Currently based in New York, I take on a select number of projects each year for clients who care about craft. Past collaborators include cultural institutions, independent publishers, luxury brands, and startups with something real to say.
