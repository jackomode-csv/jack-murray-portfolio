Leaving Queens

A self-portrait that refuses to picture the self — a body became a shadow, and the shadow moved on into a new world.

Designer — Jack Murray·2023·Photography, self-portrait·Series of four

The Brief

Make a self-portrait that cannot simply be a photo of the self. The answer was to step out of frame and leave only a cast shadow — a presence read by its outline, the body inferred rather than shown.

Concept

The shadow becomes the subject — a portrait of leaving rather than of likeness. Across the series it slips from familiar Queens ground toward somewhere unnamed, the figure dissolving into the act of departure.

“The shadow moved on, into a new world.”
